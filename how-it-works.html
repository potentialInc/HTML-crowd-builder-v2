How Crowd Builder Works

Turn great ideas into funded ventures through the power of community. We bridge the gap between visionary founders and everyday investors.

From Idea to Exit

Our transparent 4-step process ensures only the highest quality projects receive funding.

1

Discover

Browse curated startup ideas submitted by our community members and vetted for basic viability.

2

Vote

Use your voting power to support promising projects. Only ideas with strong community backing move forward.

3

Invest

Contribute capital to ideas that reach the funding threshold. Your investment is secured in escrow.

4

Grow

Track portfolio performance via your dashboard and receive returns when projects exit or pay dividends.

Community-Driven

The wisdom of the crowd vets every project. Democratic voting ensures that capital flows to the ideas with the highest potential, not just those with connections.

Transparent Process

Full visibility into how funds are allocated and used. Startups must provide regular updates and financial reports to unlock subsequent funding tranches.

Secure Platform

We use bank-grade security protocols to protect your investments and personal data. All funds are held in regulated escrow accounts until deployment.
