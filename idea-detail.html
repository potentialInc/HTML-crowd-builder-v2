About this idea

We live in an age of constant distraction. Traditional project management tools are great for listing what needs to be done, but terrible at telling you when to do it based on your actual capacity.

FocusFlow is an AI-driven project management ecosystem that automatically prioritizes your tasks based on your energy levels, calendar availability, and historical work patterns. It doesn't just manage your tasks; it manages your attention.

Unlike Jira or Trello, which require manual grooming, FocusFlow uses a background agent to suggest the optimal "Deep Work" blocks on your calendar. It integrates seamlessly with Google Calendar and Slack to block distractions when you are in a flow state.
