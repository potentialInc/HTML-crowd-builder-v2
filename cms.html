Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about investing and building with Crowd Builder.

What is Crowd Builder?

Crowd Builder is a community-owned platform where users can submit ideas, vote on the best ones, and collaboratively invest in building them. We bridge the gap between ideation and execution by pooling resources.

How does the voting system work?

Every verified user gets 10 voting credits per week. You can allocate these credits to any project in the "Discovery" phase. Projects that reach a threshold of 1,000 votes within 30 days move to the "Funding" phase.

Is my investment safe?

All investments are held in escrow via Stripe Connect until the project funding goal is met. If a project fails to meet its goal, 100% of funds are returned to your wallet. Please review our Risk Disclosure document for more details.

Can I withdraw my funds?

Yes, you can withdraw unallocated funds from your wallet at any time. Funds currently committed to a project in the "Funding" phase cannot be withdrawn until the campaign concludes.
