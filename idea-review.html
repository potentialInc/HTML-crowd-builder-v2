The Story

EcoTrack Analytics helps businesses and individuals understand their environmental impact through automated data collection and machine learning. By connecting directly with utility providers and transaction data, we provide a real-time dashboard of carbon emissions that is both accurate and actionable.

Key features include: Real-time carbon tracking, AI recommendations for reduction, Community challenges to foster engagement, and comprehensive reporting tools for ESG compliance. Our goal is to make sustainability measurable and manageable for everyone.

We believe that what gets measured gets managed. With EcoTrack, we are bringing enterprise-grade analytics to the mass market, empowering a new generation of eco-conscious consumers and businesses to make informed decisions.
