Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Crowd Builder, investing, and managing your account.

General Questions

What is Crowd Builder?
Crowd Builder is a decentralized, crowd-owned investment platform. We allow our community to propose, vet, and fund startup ideas collectively. By pooling resources, we democratize access to early-stage venture capital opportunities.
How does the voting system work?
Every registered member receives voting power proportional to their contribution history. Ideas are submitted to the "Queue," and projects that receive enough positive votes move to the funding stage. This ensures only high-quality projects vetted by the crowd are funded.
Who can submit ideas?
Any verified user can submit a project proposal. Submissions require a detailed business plan, budget breakdown, and a small submission fee (to prevent spam) which is refunded if the project reaches the voting threshold.

Investment & Funding

How do I vote for an idea?
Navigate to the "Proposals" dashboard. You can review pitch decks and financial models there. Click "Upvote" to pledge your support. You have a limited number of votes per cycle, so choose wisely.
What happens when an idea gets funded?
Once a project meets its funding goal, the capital is held in escrow. Funds are released in tranches based on milestone completion. As an investor, you receive equity tokens representing your stake in the project.
Are there any fees?
Crowd Builder charges a 2% platform fee on successfully funded projects to cover operational costs. There are no fees to create an account or browse proposals.

Account Management

How do I create an account?
Click the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner. You'll need to provide a valid email address and create a password. You can also sign up using your Google or GitHub account.
Can I delete my account?
Yes, you can request account deletion from your Settings page. Please note that if you have active investments, you may need to liquidate or transfer them before the account can be fully closed.
How do I update my profile?
Navigate to Settings > Profile. There you can update your display name, bio, notification preferences, and linked financial accounts.

Security & Privacy

Is my data secure?
We use bank-grade 256-bit encryption for all data in transit and at rest. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.
How is my identity verified?
We partner with a third-party identity provider to perform KYC (Know Your Customer) checks. This usually involves uploading a photo of your government ID and a selfie. This process is required by law for all investment platforms.
What payment methods are accepted?
We currently accept ACH bank transfers, wire transfers for large amounts, and major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex).
